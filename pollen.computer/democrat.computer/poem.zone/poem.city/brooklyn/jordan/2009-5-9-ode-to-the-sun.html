Ode to the sun

 

May 20th, 2009

Ode to the sun
the fixed hanging star
It is to you that we owe life to
and as darkness falls here
somewhere not far off
there is daytime on which is predicated by another


Forever realized by nothing but ash and electricity,
water and sunlight and my paper
ground of being
rest in certainty


never stop, never sleep
the night never reaches you
far off you hold us in place
and only the moon's shadow blocks you


As I stand in the penumbra-
for a moment you fail to be
yet your gravity, your radiation,
it reaches me
