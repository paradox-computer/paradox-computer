August 28th 2010?

the waves across the surface
like ripples are on water you are those pulsations on them


known like wind is known but organized unlike wind
you are - all its effect, its force
And so, but unlike all the air
in which you are suspended like words, thoughts-


you are by air's side in movement
its touch by your tongue becomes spoken
as like differentiated air parts cool by warm


you move like wind's arrangement with the storm cloud-
with the blue sky,
- with the cool night and sea


- structured more highly than that chaos, brings you cool breeze
is your clear mind
there that spells it.


yet why not go on just some longer
cross the surface, water, by which waves on your arrival
