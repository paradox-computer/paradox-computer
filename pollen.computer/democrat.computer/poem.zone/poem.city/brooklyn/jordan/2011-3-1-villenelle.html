Villanelle:

 

March 1, 2011

 I forget this then
 I stood in stark amazement
 because I forgot this then and then you


 how could feeling time appear so and
 how the world presses down
I forgot this then


  voice, your words let them sound
I   long for them, to hear more
  because I forgot this then and then you


  a warning came
   as leaving for that horizon
 I forgot this then


  living for a moment to be
  time moves on I know
  because I forgot this then and then you


  close up
  all the more because
  I forgot this then
  because i forgot this then and then you
