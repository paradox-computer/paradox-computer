The Money: Go

 

February 2, 2017

just take the cash and run:


we wrapped the money to keep the silver fish from eating the paper

one the boss was dead the money was ...


why could he just take a truck load himself and move to some foreign place,


why didn’t the money go with the family in suitcases to foreign lands distant places,


just take the cash and run:


we wrapped the money to keep the silver fish from eating the paper

one the booze was dead the money was …


why could he just take a truck load himself and move to some foreign place,


why didn’t the money go with the family in suitcases to foreign lands distant places,


alcohol animates life

doors slam shut

and wood can only erode

sounds move around sideways under

echo.
