Untitled

 

November 1, 2010

I got the pills to wake up -their in
the cupboard by the sink over there
go and take 'em!


I got the dreams all lined up
not quite ready let them sit,
they need it


I'm not afraid. I'm okay


I won't say any longer bad things
a lone leaf falling little turns to the ground
among its cohort it will piece to piece again
cycles through life, cycles through life and you'll come up again
but that's just the timeless road
cycle through and become a forgetful thing
