Woolgathering

 

2009

Wool-meeting


She left me breathless in the morning
when I woke up
all -but only with a touch of her lips
- to find, it has been alone the feeling of a dream


What cannot be left up to be imagined?
For how -she alters me and predicates the night and darkness


The chemical that clouds my thoughts,
  -She, -the rain that predicates the clouds
   -She is the rain, my lover
splash colors vibrate music   -radiate intensity so physically reactive
   trusting not the world
   starving and cold
   -trampled and cut by ridiculous notions


So be it that I worry not
the beauty of my love is so
  -en-grained
never understanding explanation
something larger did dispose
that which causes to believe
how a touch of love does feel-
we will send philosophy to ashes-
So that love may brush our lips
