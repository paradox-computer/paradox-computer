Dream- exit:

 

May 28th, 2009

How with you I long to be
-fade not into other shadows?
Make a dream of life as dream at night
for when I awaken you are not there


Make life a dream so when I wake
I need not dream to have you
dream to lose, to steal, to find
When only I awaken having lost something
some impression which hardly compels me to
Jump up
Wake up
out of bed into cold morning climb
sometimes I race to sleep so that I can deceive myself


Can’t thou see my heart ache
-feel my darkest fear?
what is empathy towards other people cannot touch the
waves across this water that need one single frequency
one amplitude to harmonize them.


Can’t thou see- even if I draw your eyes
you need not eyes to understand
For I don’t see you and you don’t see me
Can’t thou hear- I will convey to you
Interoperate and orate for you
All I need is for you to be not but a figure of speech
Speak to me!
Move with me like autumn day light plays and dances with the colors
the enriching of the leaves
So, show me colors
So, pass like seasons and only fortify them
collaborate to signify their happening
so one season follows from another-
when there is no need to look back
no need for sorrow until the last day
For when aligned right, each day happens
only so to be a platform of and for the next step
on a great move forward
