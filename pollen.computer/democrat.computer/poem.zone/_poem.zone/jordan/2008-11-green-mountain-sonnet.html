Green mountain sonnet

 

Winter 2008

Take this as an invitation
a digital communication
sent across electric ear lobes

analog john von neumann liquid-crystal machine-display


Clear your sensory receptor organ
vestibule of the ear
charge capacity- shock
bellows and controlled arranged supplied with air


Out of thin air prepare to hear me
compressed air my welcome guest
by indirect request
I am glad to hand you my hospitality


These words cannot fail to reach you
as I send them out over airways and
every line, vibrating antenna, orbiting celestial bodies


You can enter through the attic, you will find me in a high room looking out
across the tops of buildings, light will rise up in the morning and empty the content of our minds
