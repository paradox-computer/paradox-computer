BEFORE I FORGET

 

December 12, 2015

Before it's all over....
before that last tear drop falls...
before my heart lays cold like a rock within my breast
and does not jump at the sight of you...
like it once did,


I want you to know...
there was a time when all I thought about ...was you,
when days and nights...blended together
to form one long ribbon of fantasies.
When your touch...could comfort me...
and excite me...at the same time
When just one soft look...
or crooked grin...
could bring me to my knees.


And so...before you win...
before the bad totally overshadows the good...
before I feel better without you, than with you...
before I forget...just how much I cared...


I want to capture this feeling
and freeze it in time.


Before it's too late...
before I forget.
