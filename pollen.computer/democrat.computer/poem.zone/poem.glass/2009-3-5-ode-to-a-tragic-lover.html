Ode to a tragic lover:

 

March 5th, 2009

When I think about you I feel happiness-
It’s like a light on, to show me your face
for a moment I am suspended


This is not the way I suspected it would go
For me it did seem you would- burst through the door
or was it a dream?
When I close my eyes and fall
you’re not here to sleep


Running fingers through your hair
  -feeling the texture of defeat
you swore the ring you'd wear and would keep


I needed you to take your shoes off
and walk into the lake
when the fires came I called your name
  -but I missed you


Now I leisure, smoke, my days
you're the ashes blow away-
All you are is ashes when I die
as I exhale your memories one last time.
