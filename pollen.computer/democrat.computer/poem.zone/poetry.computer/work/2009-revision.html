Revision

 

2009

Solidify the air
make it crystalline
shapes, light and time


Stop all motion, all vibration
still the world is silent
for the first time
define all shapes so clear to vision


Draw symbols in the sand
enshrine with tokens
manipulate them
by their arrangement
generating laws, the rules that govern
reverberate an earthquake


Command the air to shatter
crack crumble,


separate the objects
the ruins of stone cities,
the fragmented statues


rearrange those blocks, in a new order
we put those pieces where they had to go
