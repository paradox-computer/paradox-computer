Phone Poem

 

July 7, 2016


baby got a place | got another heart beat
I heard it pounding in the dark then
but could not find it any longer


baby I got away
to feel another heart beat
I fell awake into his arms and


and I never thought it anyway but


I would have waited here unto his heart beat


I would have waited till he found out
I don’t want him any longer


But I would have said just listen to this heart beat
You could have longed and followed all them


And never really even known it
Even though it was the strongest we don’t need it any longer


It's just like dreaming it's just like all them. They don't really understand I'm on the beat

never thought I would be
Can't crawl away from in your arms and
Cannot fight it any longe
