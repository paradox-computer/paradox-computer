Embossed Paper Lion:

 

2008

Knotted twisted ferociously engaged and intertwined
a single chord and sound unheard tones resound


-who has a story intertwined with glory, triumph, poverty and war;
   -lonely childhood sadden; hearts heavy hearts broken, fractured
   -and torn


-who lost hope and found harmony admits the streets and people
   -no- longer alone.
-who still brush away tears fractured, broken dreams and hopes.


Still burning with life a beacon of great strength
and fervor echoed and resounded before I saw you as a lion
when no more on the plains existed


Strong and beautiful seemingly not afraid, in comparison, but with
the same story, same fears and roar.
