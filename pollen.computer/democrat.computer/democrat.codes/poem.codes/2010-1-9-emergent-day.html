Emergent day:

 

January 9, 2010

  -come forth
be triumphant
my fair heart
say that I do not treat you badly
awake and be silent and wait for a moment
long winters long winters long winters
go by slowly
go shiver


come back dawn
I woke up too late
I could have dreamt longer but your light shines so
here I am awake
just before the sun sets again


be it fair, sunlight, that I did trade you for a cool dream
for I wake now only hardly to remember
all's echos and earthquakes
untimely reverberations
no more hardly a story than just some crazy happening
